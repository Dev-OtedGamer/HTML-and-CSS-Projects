Steps to Traversing the Magical Portal:

  1. Collect the Sneakers:

    • Be at the Docks in Dansville between 3:10 pm(Game Time) and 4:15 pm(Game Time) to meet with Stan.
      Don't be late!
    • Look for Tom in Oak Forest near the city of Kanden at 2:15 am(Game Time), he is uasually
      around there. Don't be late!(Spoiler: Dont open the packages or you will have to start over after server reset.)
    • Go back to the Docks in Dansville between 3:10 pm(Game Time) and 4:15 pm(Game Time) to meet with Stan.
      Don't be late!
      (This next part is a little tricky and you may need help and gold for fast travel)
    • Lydia follows a random schedule and at 4:20 am(Game Time) can be found in 1 of 3 places. Don't be late!
      1. South Landing
      2. Port West
      3. Seal Rock Crossing
    • Go back to the Docks in Dansville between 3:10 pm(Game Time) and 4:15 pm(Game Time) to meet with Stan.
      Don't be late!

  2. Get the Sneakers Blessed:

    • Be at Fathers Rock at Midnight 12:00 am(Game Time) to meet with Friar John
    • The flower can be found in the Field of Despair between 10:00 pm(Game Time) and 4:30 am(Game Time)
    • Go back to Fathers Rock at Midnight 12:00 am(Game Time) to meet with Friar John
    • The Rabbit can be found in the Valley of Mist between 5:30 am(Game Time) and 7:30 am(Game Time)
    • Go back to Fathers Rock at Midnight 12:00 am(Game Time) to meet with Friar John
    • The Water needs to be collected between 8:30 pm(Game Time) and 9:30 pm(Game Time) at Deadman Falls
      in order to catch the Bioluminescent Dinoflagellates
    • Go back to Fathers Rock at Midnight 12:00 am(Game Time) to meet with Friar John

Relevant information:

  • A game day is 4 hours real time, 1 hour game time is 10 minutes real time.
  • If you spend Stan's gold, you will still owe him.(It does not reset with server reset)
  • Lydia only changes area at server reset
  • Server reset is at 12:00 am Pacific Standard Time(PT)
  • Premium Account is required in order to fast travel
  • This Quest can only be completed once by each player
