Achieving Security and Safety with Access Control

In todays day and age we all would like a little peace of mind when it comes to our Safety and Security especially when it comes to our loved ones.
Access Control is one way of achieving that goal. With todays advancements in technology there is a wide variety of systems and many types of RFID chips (Radio Frequency Identification) and the items that house these chips. For instance Cards/Badges, Key Fobs, Rings, Watches etc.. There are many applications and ways to implement these systems added with a well orginized and documented key inventory system either in small scale Office buildings/Apartment bulings or in large scacle Government Facilities/Manufacturing Facilities.

Visual Presentation for Access Control

This is a visual presentation I created to show a way of Visual Inventory Management for Access Control points. I have incorporated both an RDIF system and Key inventory with additional Door location. The 3D model is in various states of completion to show the different amounts of detail from simple to more detailed. I used Blender 3.6 to create the 3D model, adding photos could be used to to accomplish the same affect I also went further and added in a few different ways to view this HTML file using CSS.
